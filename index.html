What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines. These systems are capable of learning, reasoning, and solving problems. AI powers tools like Siri, Google Assistant, and self-driving cars.

Types of AI

There are mainly three types of AI: Narrow AI, General AI, and Superintelligent AI. Most AI today is narrow but powerful in specific tasks.

Popular AI Tools

Popular AI tools include ChatGPT, DALL·E, Grammarly, and Midjourney. These tools help in writing, designing, coding, and even marketing.
